About Me

I am a Ph.D student in School of Electronics Engineering and Computer Science at Pekings University as well as a member of Key Laboratory of Machine Perception (Peking University), supervised by Prof. Hongyan Li.

I received my BEng in School of Electronics and Information at Northwestern Polytechnical University in June 2018. My reasearch is focused on weakly supervised learning. I am especially interested in partial label learning, evidence theory and fuzzy set theory.
